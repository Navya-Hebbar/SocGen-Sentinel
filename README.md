# SocGen-Sentinel 
SocGen Sentinel is an AI-powered Vendor Risk Intelligence Platform designed to help organizations identify, assess, and monitor third-party risks.

The platform provides:

- Vendor risk scoring
- Compliance assessment (SOC2, GDPR, ISO27001)
- Breach impact analysis
- Contract and certification monitoring
- AI-generated risk explanations
- Executive dashboards and risk analytics

By combining machine learning, risk intelligence, and compliance monitoring, SocGen Sentinel enables organizations to proactively manage vendor risks and improve audit readiness.

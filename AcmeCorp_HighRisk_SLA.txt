MASTER SERVICE AGREEMENT (MSA)

This Master Service Agreement is entered into by and between SocGen Sentinel (Client) and AcmeCorp (Vendor).

Section 1. Services
AcmeCorp shall provide cloud hosting services as outlined in Appendix A.

Section 2. Data Processing
The Vendor will process client data. Vendor reserves the right to share data with third-party subprocessors without notifying the Client or obtaining prior written consent.

Section 3. Security Requirements
The Vendor is not required to maintain SOC 2 Type II compliance or undergo annual penetration testing. Standard encryption protocols will be applied on a best-effort basis.

Section 4. Limitation of Liability
The Vendor's maximum liability for any data breach, regardless of negligence, shall not exceed $100. Furthermore, Vendor is not responsible for notification of data breaches to end users or paying for credit monitoring services.

Section 5. Termination
Either party may terminate this agreement with 24 hours notice.
